Brownie Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Brownie is a desktop app that transforms clipboard text locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains how Brownie handles information.

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Information Accessed or Collected

Brownie may access the following information to provide its features:

  • Text that you copy to the clipboard
  • Brownie app settings
  • Local diagnostic information used to check app behavior or investigate issues

Brownie does not collect user accounts, names, email addresses, location data, contacts, photos, camera data, microphone data, payment information, or health information.

How Information Is Used

Brownie uses accessed information only for the following purposes:

  • To transform text from the clipboard
  • To display clipboard history inside the app
  • To save user settings
  • To help verify app stability

Storage and Transmission

Brownie does not send clipboard content or transformation results to external servers.

Clipboard history is kept locally while the app is running and is cleared when the app exits.

Settings and diagnostic information may be stored locally on the user's device.

Sharing with Third Parties

Brownie does not sell, share, or disclose users' personal information, clipboard content, or transformation results to third parties.

Brownie does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

User Controls

Users can change or reset Brownie settings from the app settings screen.

To delete locally stored settings or diagnostic information, uninstall Brownie or delete the app data from the operating system.

Children's Privacy

Brownie is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Brownie features are added or changed.
